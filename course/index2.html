Overview

Purpose

Currently, biostatistics is hard for people who do not like math. Especially for people working on health, biostatistics seems like an awful puzzle game. My purpose is to softly teach the basic biostatistics concepts such as population, sample, sampling, probability distributions, hypothesis tests, and so forth by using simple information technology resources so that the learning is maximized.

Audience

This course is towards all people who want to learn biostatistics such as doctors, physicstherapeutics, psichologists, nutritionists, biologystis, scientists, and anyone who desire to aquire this kind of knowledgement. The majority of the biostatistics courses have been teaching by using boring resources and/or hard language. My course is based on experimental biostatistics thanks the computational simulation that creates clever online trials where the student can assessment herself or himself. The virtual laboratory is compunded by a lot of virtual experiments. Although, each experiment reflects the reality because it was developed by randomness. All classes are watched by any personal computer, cell phone, lap-top, and so forth.
